## Grouping?

Scientists group organisms to better understand the vast diversity of life. This process ‚Äî called _classification_ ‚Äî helps us recognize patterns, make predictions, and study relationships.

Organisms can be grouped in many ways: by **size**, by shared **features**, or by how closely **related** they are.

- **Taxonomy** ‚Äî the science of naming, describing, and classifying organisms
- **Phylogeny** ‚Äî the study of evolutionary relationships among organisms
